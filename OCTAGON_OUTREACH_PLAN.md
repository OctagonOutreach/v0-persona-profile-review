### **7. FAQ SECTION**
**Goal:** Answer common objections + build confidence

**Likely FAQs:**

1. **"How do you define '40 qualified students'?"**
   - Answer: Qualified leads who are genuinely interested in joining your gym, as tracked through conversion pixels, phone calls, and demo class sign-ups. We don't count traffic or clicks—only real prospects.

2. **"What if my gym is in a small/rural area?"**
   - Answer: The strategy adapts to your market size. We've built models that work for urban gyms, small towns, and suburban areas. The core system is the same; the targeting is hyper-localized.

3. **"How much time do I need to invest?"**
   - Answer: You need about 2-3 hours per week to help optimize messaging and provide feedback. We handle the technical heavy lifting. Your job is to keep the gym running and convert students we send.

4. **"Do you work with other gyms besides MMA?"**
   - Answer: Currently, we're laser-focused on MMA gyms. This is where I have personal expertise and deep conviction. That might change later, but right now, MMA is the only space I'm taking on.

5. **"What if we don't see results by Week 8?"**
   - Answer: We adjust immediately. We analyze data, test new ad angles, refine messaging, and scale what's working. The beauty of the system is it's flexible and data-driven. We don't just hope—we optimize constantly.

6. **"How do you handle platform changes (Facebook algorithm updates, Google changes)?"**
   - Answer: The core system is built on fundamentals that outlast any algorithm update: clear messaging, targeted audiences, and continuous optimization. We stay ahead of platform changes so you don't have to worry about it.

7. **"Can I cancel early if I want?"**
   - Answer: Yes, but the guarantee only applies if we complete the full 90 days. Early cancellation means you forfeit the money-back guarantee.

**Design:**
- Accordion style (click to expand/collapse)
- Each question in a collapsed state by default
- Red accent line on expanded answers
- Subtle background color (light gray or dark variant)
